Holiday Market

The magical spirit of Holiday Market turns your matches to gold! Win a prize when you uncover three matching symbols. Watch for the special Gold Ornaments to award you a bonusgame: trigger the Wreath Bonus to spin for a win or trigger the Cabin Bonus to open doors to prizes!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

Press each ornament to reveal symbols. Reveal 3 matching symbols to win the corresponding prize.

Reveal the Cabin Bonus Gold Ornament to trigger the Cabin Bonus. Select doors to reveal prizes. Reveal COLLECT to end the bonus.

Reveal the Wreath Bonus Gold Ornament to trigger the Wreath Bonus. Spin the wheel and win the amount highlighted when the wheel stops.

Any awarded bonus games are played after all symbols in the base game have been revealed.

The game ends when all symbols and bonus items are revealed.

Dismiss the win message and view the game’s outcome by pressing the message.

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to play the game to completion automatically. Game play continues until all selections have been made and any prizes have been awarded.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Bonus wins are added to total winning prize values.

Wins display as currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.

In this game, the wheel is a graphical animation for entertainment purposes only and does not represent an actual wheel. The chances of obtaining a specific segment are therefore not equiprobable.
